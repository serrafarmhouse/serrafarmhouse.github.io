About us

Serra Farm House is surrounded by coffee plantation, pepper vines, melodious chirping of birds and crickets, mesmerizes with lush green trees and lake. It’s a perfect gateway for people looking for a serene and calm place to enjoy their vacation in Coorg. A Simple and comfortable destination offering the warmth of a home and basic conveniences. We are not offering you the luxury of hotels, the ambience where you are made to feel like a guest. However, we are inviting you to feel the culture, tradition and hospitality of Coorg, to give you a feeling of what it’s like to be a part of our family.

Our farm house is situated approximately 260 km from Bangalore, 30km from Kushalnagar and it takes around 5 km from Madikeri Town.
